---
title: Introduction
description: How foreign-trained dentists can become licensed to practice in the U.S.
---

In most states, foreign-trained dentists must earn a degree from a CODA-accredited
dental education program to qualify for licensure and practice in the United States.
**Some exceptions exist, which I'll explain later in this guide.**

U.S. dental schools typically admit international students through two main tracks:
**Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) programs** and **residency programs.**
